Our Story: Passion, Progress, People.

Ryan Sports Club was founded on the belief that world-class facilities and a supportive community are the keys to unlocking athletic potential. Learn about our journey and the values that drive us.

Meet the Team

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional, comprehensive sports and wellness environment that inspires individuals, families, and teams to achieve peak physical performance, lifelong health, and community connection. We are committed to maintaining elite facilities, fostering expert coaching, and promoting an inclusive atmosphere where every member feels supported in their journey.

"Excellence is not an act, but a habit. We build habits."

Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding fairness, honesty, and transparency in all club operations.
  • Excellence: Committing to the highest standards in facilities, coaching, and service.
  • Community: Fostering an inclusive, motivating, and welcoming environment for everyone.

Club History & Milestones

2010: The Vision is Formed

Ryan Sports Club is officially founded by the Ryan Family on the grounds of a former city park, with a focus on comprehensive indoor basketball and volleyball.

2015: Phase II Expansion

Introduction of the 50-meter Olympic Pool and the FIFA-regulation outdoor soccer field, transforming the club into a premier multi-sport facility.

2019: Launch of Wellness Center

Opened the dedicated two-story Fitness & Wellness Center, including the spa, sauna, and dedicated yoga/spin studios for holistic health services.

2023: Hosting First National Event

Hosted the National College Volleyball Championship, validating the quality of our facilities and attracting international recognition.
